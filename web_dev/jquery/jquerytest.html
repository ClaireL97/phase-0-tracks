Erased Summary


Erased is a story about a guy who has the power to go back in time before a problem. He can't control it and it is always before something big. He ends up going back in time after he is framed for a crime he didn't commit. The result changes the future but he has to stop events from happening in the past. He is a 29 year old with all the knowlege back in his 5th grade self. He has to save people in the past to change the future where his mom dies.
